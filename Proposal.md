# DreamPath: Business & Strategy Proposal

## 1. Executive Summary
**DreamPath** is an AI-powered personal growth application designed to bridge the gap between ambitious dreaming and daily execution. By combining generative AI, gamification, and a professional monetization framework via RevenueCat, DreamPath provides a structured path for users to turn their life goals into actionable 4-week roadmaps.

## 2. The Problem
Many individuals, particularly within the ambitious 18-35 demographic, suffer from "Dream Paralysis." They have significant goals (career pivots, solo travel, financial independence) but lack a concrete starting point. Statistics show that 92% of people who set New Year's resolutions fail to achieve them, primarily due to:
*   **Vague Planning**: Goals are too broad ("Get fit" vs "5-min stretch today").
*   **Overwhelm**: The distance between current reality and the dream feels insurmountable.
*   **Lack of Accountability**: No system to track small daily wins.

## 4. The Solution: DreamPath
DreamPath solves these issues through a three-pillar approach:
1.  **AI Roadmapping**: Instant decomposition of any dream into micro-tasks (under 15 minutes).
2.  **Psychological Gamification**: The "Jar of Dreams" provides visual "variable rewards" for checking off tasks, fostering long-term habit formation.
3.  **Social Sharing & Accountability**: Built-in support for sharing progress and roadmaps to social media platforms, leveraging community support for motivation.
4.  **Mindset Coaching**: Context-aware content (Pep Talks, Tips) from established community leaders (like Gabby Beckford) to combat "Imposter Syndrome."

## 5. Monetization Strategy
